About Me

I am a first generation daughter to immigrant parents from India. Growing up, I learned of American holidays and traditions through school and friends, but I never actually got the chance to partake. When I met my husband, he brought to his home and shared his culture with me. Through the years and the inclusion of my parents, we created new traditions.
India is a subcontinent, so each State has its own specialty foods. My family is from the Maharastrian State of India, so during Thanksgiving, we make foods we love to eat during Diwali!
